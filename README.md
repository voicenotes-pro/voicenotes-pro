Some features developed in this:

|Feature                      |Description                                                                           
|🎙 **Live Transcription**     |Real-time speech-to-text using your browser’s built-in Web Speech API                 |
|👥 **Speaker Identification** |Automatically labels different speakers (A, B, C) based on speech pauses              |
|🤖 **AI Summary**             |Uses Claude AI to summarize key points, decisions, and topics                         |
|✅ **Action Items**           |Extracts tasks, owners, and deadlines spoken during the recording                     |
|💬 **Sentiment Tagging**      |Highlights positive and negative language in real time                                |
|🏷 **Auto-Categorization**    |Classifies recordings as Meeting, Brainstorm, Client Call, Interview, or Personal Note|
|📅 **Deadline Extraction**    |Pulls out any dates or timeframes mentioned in the recording                          |
|📝 **Custom Vocabulary**      |Pre-define company terms, names, and acronyms for better accuracy                     |
|⚠️ **Confidence Flagging**    |Underlines words the speech engine wasn’t confident about                             |
|☁️ **Google Drive Export**    |Save transcripts, summaries, and action items directly to your Drive                  |
|📦 **Local Download Fallback**|No Drive? Export everything as a `.txt` file instantly
